# Quick Start: Open Source Project Overview

## What is this project?

**JUCE Plugin Generator Studio** is a free, open-source tool for creating audio plugins without manual configuration.

---

## Common Questions

### Q: Is this free?
**A:** Yes! MIT License means free for personal and commercial use.

### Q: Do I have to pay if I sell plugins made with this?
**A:** No! You can sell plugins. Just include the license notice.

### Q: What if the tool breaks my DAW?
**A:** You use it at your own risk. See [DISCLAIMER.md](DISCLAIMER.md).

### Q: What if my generated plugin has bugs?
**A:** That's your responsibility. Test thoroughly before release.

### Q: Can I modify the tool?
**A:** Yes! MIT License allows modifications.

### Q: Do I have to share my modifications?
**A:** No, but we'd love if you contributed improvements back!

### Q: Is this for beginners?
**A:** Sort of. You need to know about audio plugins and C++ to use generated code.

### Q: What's the "alpha" status mean?
**A:** It's still in early development. May have bugs. Not guaranteed stable.

### Q: How do I report a bug?
**A:** Open a GitHub issue with the details in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

### Q: Will you support me?
**A:** Best-effort community support only. No professional support.

### Q: How do I contribute?
**A:** See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for detailed instructions.
